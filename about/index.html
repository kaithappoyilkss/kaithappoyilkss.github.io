STORY OF KAITHAPPOYIL KSHEEROLPADAKA SAHAKARANA SANGHAM

Add info Add info

Kaithappoyil Co- operative Society established in 1959. It is situated in a small village Kaithappoyil. Society was registered on 28th December 1959 and started operation on 9th May 1960.Now, there are 2035 members and out of this, there are 90 milk pouring members. Average quantity of milk pouring is 800 liters/day.

Our exclusive Activities

Activities

A Milk based production unit is working under the society which produces curd. Providing cattle feed, feed supplements, paddy straw, green fodder in subsidized price. Cattle insurance, vet medical camp, farmers training programs. Provide modern infrastructure facilities for procurement chilling and supplying of milk

Recognitions

Quality of milk and better price to framers

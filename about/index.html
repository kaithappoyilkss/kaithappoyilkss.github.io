STORY OF OMASSERY KSHEEROLPADAKA SAHAKARANA SANGHAM

Add info Add info

Omassery ksheerolpadaka sahakarana sangham is a village level primary dairy co-operative society. The society was registered under the Department of Dairy Development, Govt. of Kerala in the year 1987 and started functioning on 1-2-1988. The society is affiliated to Milma – Malabar regional Union. The area of operation of the society is Omassery Grama Panchayat. The management of the society is vested in the managing committee directly elected from milk Pouring members of the society. The farmer membership stood at 655 as on 31-3-2018. The milk procurement has shown a growth from 66640 liters during 1988-89 to 532461 liters in 2017-18. The society has concentrated on the socio economic development of dairy farmers by utilizing own fund and financial help received from Department of Dairy Development, Milma and grama Panchayat. The society also providing other support like cattle feed, feed supplements, paddy straw, green fodder, Veterinary service, cattle insurance, veterinary medical camps. Besides this the society provide medical insurance, accident insurance to the dairy farmers. About 100 farmers are getting monthly pension of Rs.1100 per month from Kerala dairy farmer’s welfare board. A Bulk milk cooler with a capacity of 5000 liters was installed in the society by Milma - Malabar union to improve the quality of milk received at society, during 2017-18 year the society procured 1720975 liters of milk from 12 primary societies through this BMC. The society also running a small scale milk product unit. Curd, buttermilk, ghee are the main byproducts. The society is concentrated on development of the milk product unit with utilizing financial assistance from dairy development department. The society is also a business correspondent of Kozhikode district co-operative bank [KDC BANK}, by this the society is providing banking facilities to dairy farmers from society office. Society running a milk booth in Omassery Panchayat bus stand, and a cattle feed store in Omassery town. Besides all these we conduct varies training programs for dairy farmers. Omassery Ksheerolpadaka Sahakarana Sangham is committed to ensuring the prosperity to dairy farmers.

Our exclusive Activities

Activities

Providing cattle feed, feed supplements, paddy straw, green fodder in subsidized price. Cattle insurance, vet medical camp, farmers training programs. Society supplies milk to 9 Govt. schools at a lower price. A small scale milk based production unit is working under the society which produces curd, butter milk, ghee. Society running a milk booth at Omassery Panchayat bus stand. Society provides banking facilities to dairy farmers at society office. Society provides assistance to farmers for setting up of dairy farm, biogas plant, milking machine etc.. Farmers get various insurance schemes such as medi-claim insurance, accident insurance, life insurance, through society. Society collects milk from 11 primary milk societies of Kozhikode district and society has a bulk milk cooler [BMC} with 5000 liters capacity.

Recognitions

Society works as a business correspondent of Kozhikode district co-op bank (KDC BANK). Quality of milk and better price to framers
